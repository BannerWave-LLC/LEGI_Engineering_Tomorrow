Why Engineering Tomorrow Labs?

Give students a new perspective on their futures with a virtual lab or lab day event. Engineering Tomorrow labs introduce students to the world of engineering with the help of expert engineers and college student mentors — all at no cost to you or your students.

  • Flexible instruction model
