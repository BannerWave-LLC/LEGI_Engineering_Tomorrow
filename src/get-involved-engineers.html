How can you get involved?

  • Deliver a Lab

    Introduce and present an engineering topic and lab activity designed by Engineering Tomorrow engineers. This is a turnkey option; you receive all materials ahead of time and your focus is on guiding and inspiring students throughout the lab or Lab Day event.

    Explore our labs
  • Develop a Lab

    If you’re passionate about a topic and want to be more involved in lab development, we offer that option as well. You’ll work with our engineers from day one to develop the presentation, brainstorm the activity, and more.

    Contact our team
  • Deliver a Keynote

    Our four-hour Lab Day events all culminate with an inspiring keynote presentation delivered by a professional engineer and expert in the lab topic. You’ll have the chance to talk about your own journey in engineering and inspire young, diverse minds to choose this career path.

    Explore Lab Day events
How our labs work
  • Part 1: Video intro
    A professional engineer introduces the lab topic and activity via a pre-recorded video.
  • Step 2: Complete lab with your students
    Students work together on your schedule to complete the activity presented by the engineer in session one.
  • Part 3: Live wrap-up
    An engineer and a STEM college student conference live with the class to react to student presentations and share their engineering journeys.
