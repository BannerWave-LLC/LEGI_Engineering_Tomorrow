Students will learn about
the process of drug delivery
in the body.

They will design a small-scale staged release drug delivery system by applying transport fundamentals via diffusion across membranes and pharmaceutical drug encapsulation.

Students will assess how effectively they can utilize various materials to encase differently colored tablets in water to stage their release of color in an order predetermined by the student.
